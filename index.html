About us

We speak the good
food language

A well-trained team handles the kitchen which produces about 50 tonnes of heat/fry-and-serve products per month. Significant investment has been made in product research and development. Today the company has 150+ outlets spread across Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, West Bengal, New Delhi, Bihar, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and a few North-Eastern states. The company has also tied up with various food delivery partners like Food Panda, Zomato, Swiggy, Roadrunnr etc., to expand the reach of its business.

Franchising Countries

SPAIN

Amidst the vibrant tapestry of culinary experiences that Spain offers, our restaurant proudly stands as a beacon of flavor, with its roots firmly planted in the rich soil of gastronomic tradition. From the sun-kissed shores of the Mediterranean to the bustling streets of Madrid, our franchise extends its reach across the Spanish landscape, offering patrons an opportunity to savor the same exquisite flavors and impeccable service that have become synonymous with our brand. Whether nestled in the heart of Barcelona's historic district or gracing the bustling boulevards of Valencia, each location remains true to our commitment to culinary excellence, delivering a dining experience that transcends borders and captivates the senses.

ITALY

Nestled amidst the rolling hills of Tuscany, the bustling streets of Rome, and the sun-drenched shores of the Amalfi Coast, our restaurant franchise proudly extends its culinary footprint to the enchanting landscapes of Italy. With each location meticulously selected to showcase the diverse flavors and traditions of Italian cuisine, our franchise offers patrons an authentic gastronomic experience that pays homage to the country's rich culinary heritage. Whether indulging in the delicate flavors of handmade pasta in Florence, savoring the robust aromas of Neapolitan pizza in Naples, or sampling the freshest seafood along the shores of Sicily, our Italian establishments promise an unforgettable journey through the tantalizing tastes of Italy. With every dish crafted with care and precision, our franchise celebrates the artistry of Italian cooking

FRANCE

In the heartland of culinary sophistication and gastronomic innovation, our restaurant franchise proudly extends its reach to the picturesque landscapes of France. Nestled amidst the charming cobblestone streets of Paris, Lyon's culinary epicenter, and the sun-drenched vineyards of Bordeaux, our presence in France is a testament to our commitment to excellence in dining. Each location, whether it be in the bustling arrondissements of Paris or the quaint villages of Provence, embodies the essence of French culinary artistry, offering patrons an unforgettable journey through the flavors and traditions that have made France a mecca for food lovers worldwide.
